Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2025

Purpose

This policy outlines acceptable usage of MiniFyn's URL shortening service. We're committed to providing reliable service while ensuring user safety and maintaining trust.

URL Content Guidelines

When using MiniFyn, don't create shortened URLs that:

  • Impersonate others or mislead users
  • Distribute malware or harmful code
  • Promote unauthorized access to systems
  • Share copyrighted content without permission
  • Link to phishing or scam websites
  • Contain pornographic or sexually explicit content
  • Promote hate speech or discrimination
  • Violate any applicable laws

Content Moderation

MiniFyn employs an automated domain blocklist system to prevent the creation of shortened URLs that point to known malicious, phishing, or otherwise harmful websites. This blocklist is regularly updated from trusted security sources.

If you encounter a MiniFyn URL that violates our guidelines, please report it using our abuse reporting form. All reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action will be taken within 48 hours of verification.

Service Usage

Users must:

  • Stay within their plan's usage limits
  • Use appropriate rate limiting for API requests
  • Maintain secure password practices
  • Report security vulnerabilities responsibly
  • Keep API credentials confidential

Metadata Handling

For free and anonymous users, MiniFyn does not fetch or store metadata from destination URLs. Paid users have the option to copy metadata (title, description, image) from destination URLs to their shortened links, but must use this feature responsibly and not in a way that misleads users about the destination content.

Business Usage

Business users should:

  • Use custom domains for branded links
  • Implement proper error handling
  • Monitor their URL performance
  • Keep their contact information updated

Enforcement

MiniFyn reserves the right to:

  • Investigate potential violations
  • Remove URLs that violate this policy within 48 hours of verification
  • Suspend or terminate accounts without prior notice
  • Cooperate with legal authorities when required
  • Block domains or IP addresses associated with repeated abuse
